Understanding Our Fabric Range

Cotton Fabric

Cotton is known for breathability and softness. Our cotton fabrics are ideal for summer and long daily wear, providing comfort even in warm climates.

Wash & Wear Fabric

Wash & Wear fabric is designed for convenience. It resists wrinkles, requires less maintenance, and is suitable for office and routine wear.

Khaddar Fabric

Khaddar is a heavier fabric commonly used in winter. Our khaddar collection offers warmth, durability, and long-lasting performance.

Boski Fabric

Boski is a premium fabric known for its smooth texture and formal appearance, suitable for special occasions.
