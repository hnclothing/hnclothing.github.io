Complete Fabric Guide

Cotton Fabric

Cotton is one of the most popular fabrics for men due to its softness and breathability. Our cotton fabrics are skin-friendly, lightweight, and ideal for summer and daily wear. They provide comfort throughout the day, even in warm weather.

Wash & Wear

Wash & Wear fabric is designed for convenience and durability. It is wrinkle-resistant, easy to maintain, and perfect for office wear and regular use. This fabric gives a clean and sharp appearance with minimal effort.

Khaddar

Khaddar is a warm and durable fabric, commonly used during winter seasons. Our khaddar collection offers comfort, thickness, and long-lasting quality, making it suitable for cold weather.

Boski

Boski is a premium fabric known for its smooth texture and elegant finish. It is commonly used for formal wear and special occasions, providing a classy and refined look.
